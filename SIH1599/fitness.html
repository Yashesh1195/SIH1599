Beginner Level (Weeks 1-3)

Week-1
Day Exercise Reps/Sets
Day 1 Full Body Strength
Push-Ups (Knee variation if needed) 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Bodyweight Squats 3 sets of 12-15 reps
Plank 3 sets, hold for 20-30 sec
Glute Bridges 3 sets of 10-12 reps
Superman Hold 2 sets of 10 reps (hold for 3 sec)
Day 2 Cardio & Endurance
Jumping Jacks 3 sets of 1 minute
High Knees 3 sets of 30 seconds
Marching in Place 2 sets of 2 minutes
Day 3 Active Rest & Flexibility
Dynamic Stretching 10-15 minutes
Yoga Poses (Child’s Pose, Cobra) 10-15 minutes
Day 4 Upper Body Strength
Push-Ups 3 sets of 10-12 reps
Wall Push-Ups 3 sets of 10-12 reps
Arm Circles 3 sets of 20 seconds
Day 5 Lower Body Strength & Core
Bodyweight Squats 3 sets of 12-15 reps
Lunges 3 sets of 10 reps per leg
Plank 3 sets, hold for 30 seconds
Leg Raises 2 sets of 12-15 reps
